# Stage 3 Evaluation Workflow

## Purpose

Stage 3 evaluates only race-day information that becomes available shortly before the race.

Stage 1 evaluates historical ability.
Stage 2 evaluates supporting condition factors.
Stage 3 evaluates today's dynamic conditions.

Stage 3 produces the **Stage 3 Race-Day Score only**.

---

## Prerequisite

Stage 3 starts only after Stage 1 and Stage 2 have been completed.

Required inputs:

- results/stage1/stage1_scores.md
- results/stage2/stage2_scores.md
- Official weather
- Official track condition
- Latest odds
- Official field information
- Race pace projection

---

## Evaluation Categories

Maximum Score: 30

### 1. Weather Suitability
Score: 0 to 5

Evaluate whether today's weather is favorable based on historical evidence.

### 2. Track Condition Suitability
Score: 0 to 8

Evaluate suitability for Firm, Good, Yielding or Soft conditions using verified race records.

### 3. Race Pace Scenario
Score: -3 to 9

Evaluate the expected pace and whether the horse's running style is advantaged or disadvantaged.
Use the projected field composition only.

### 4. Latest Odds
Score: -2 to 4

Evaluate only the information implied by the latest betting market.
Do not determine betting value or betting strategy.
Large market support or significant market weakness may be considered as supplementary evidence only.

### 5. Race-day Factors
Score: -2 to 4

Evaluate official race-day information not already scored above, such as late scratches or officially confirmed operational changes.
Do not use paddock impressions or subjective comments.

---

## Prohibited Information

Never use:

- Betting strategy
- Expected value
- Final prediction
- Race marks
- Unsupported speculation

---

## Output

Create:

results/stage3/XX_horse_name.md
results/stage3/stage3_scores.md
results/stage3/stage3_ranking.md

Stage 3 is complete only after every qualified horse has been evaluated using the current policy.
